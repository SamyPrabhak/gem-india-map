# Interactive Jewelry Map  
# 💎 Jewels of India 

An interactive map exploring India's rich jewelry traditions.

## Overview

This project visualizes the diverse jewelry ecosystem across India, including all states, union territories, and islands (Lakshadweep and Andaman & Nicobar Islands). Click on any region to discover its unique ornamental heritage.

## Why This Matters

**Image searches for "Indian Jewelry" were flat worldwide for 4 years. Then in 2026 — they spiked to all-time highs.**
**The $95B market is going global, and the world is paying attention.**

## Built With

- [Lovable](https://lovable.dev) — AI-powered development platform
- Interactive SVG mapping
- Regional jewelry data and imagery

## Features

- **Complete Coverage** — All states, territories, and **especially island regions**
- **Interactive Design** — Click to explore each region's traditions
- **Rich Context** — Discover techniques, materials, and cultural significance
- **Islands Included** — Lakshadweep and Andaman & Nicobar representation
